What We Believe

Cornerstone Delaware Tamil Prayer Fellowship is a Bible-believing, non-denominational church. We hold to the authority of Scripture, the lordship of Jesus Christ, and the power of the Holy Spirit in guiding believers.

We believe in salvation through Jesus Christ alone, in the importance of prayer, worship, and discipleship, and in spreading the Gospel to our local community and beyond.
